Features

Search the way your brain thinks

Not by filename. Not by date. By what's actually in the file.

🧠

AI Semantic Search

Search your files by meaning instead of filenames. Ask "photo of my dog at a park" and find it instantly — even if it's named IMG_4821.jpg.

Powered by Gemini Embeddings
🎨

Multimodal Search

Search across images, videos, audio, PDFs, Word docs, code files — all in one place.

⚡

Real-Time Indexing

Files become searchable automatically. Add a document, it's in your search results within seconds.

👁️

Instant Preview

See image thumbnails, video previews, and text snippets before opening a single file.

🔒

Privacy First

Everything runs locally on your machine. Your files never leave your computer. Only embeddings are computed remotely.

🤖

AI Ask Mode

Ask questions about your files. "What do my PDFs say about transformers?" — get an AI-generated answer from your own content.

How It Works

Simple pipeline. Powerful results.

Search Wizard processes your files locally and makes them instantly queryable with AI.

01
📂

Index Your Files

Choose folders to index. Search Wizard crawls and reads your documents, images, videos, and audio.

→
02
⚙️

Extraction

Content is extracted from each file — text from PDFs, frames from videos, spectrograms from audio.

→
03
🧬

Embeddings

Google Gemini converts each chunk into a 768-dimensional semantic vector stored in FAISS.

→
04
🔍

Vector Search

Your query is embedded and matched against your file vectors at lightning speed.

→
05
✅

Instant Results

Top results surface with previews, ranked by semantic similarity to your query.
